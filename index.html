Welcome to Sea Monster!

Sea Monster Crowdfunding is a pioneering platform dedicated to supporting independent game developers and bringing innovative gaming experiences to life. Since our founding 12 years ago, we have been committed to bridging the gap between talented indie developers and gaming enthusiasts who want to support groundbreaking projects. Our mission is to provide a space where creative minds can showcase their unique game concepts and receive the funding they need to turn their visions into reality. We believe that every great game deserves a chance to reach its audience, regardless of the size of the development team or budget, and we're proud to have helped countless developers launch their dream projects through our community-powered funding platform.
